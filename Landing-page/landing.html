Get the important things done

In our daily lives, we found that sometimes we lack motivation to get the important things in life done. One of the main reasons for this is because no one is keeping us accountable, there is not enough pressure. This inspired us to create our application - candy. Candy challenge is a platform for people to interact with others by doing challenge. As covid-19 influence the world, people get few and few social activity. We provide a special way to let people get in touch with sharing those challenges and share the progress.
